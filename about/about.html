About Vianca

And why she was chosen

When I was 13 my mother told me she was going to have another child. Being the only child my whole life I thought: "what a terrible idea, you already have me". But then Vianca happened.

She was a special child, and still is. She is stubborn, but she's also determined. A little too extroverted, but only with anyone who's not her immediate family.

She's become my best friend. But she would tell you otherwise.

I decided to dedicate this website to her. Vianca is just now starting her life. I think having this record of what her life was the moment she passed the threshold into adulthood will help put in perspective what life is about.

And maybe in the future she'll use it to show her children how terrible I was at designing.
